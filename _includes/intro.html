This project aims to simplify the process of creating and updating apps and other payloads in Intune by leveraging the power of AutoPkg. With AutoPkg, you can automate the process of downloading, packaging, and uploading apps to Intune, saving you time and effort.


Found on this site are recipes specifically created for Intune, sourced from the almenscorner-recipes repo. You can either contribute your own recipes to this repository or build your own based on over 10K recipes available in the AutoPkg recipe repositories.
